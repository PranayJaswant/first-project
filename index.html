Family Dog Breed

1.Golden Retriever

Description: Known for their gentle temperament, intelligence, and patience. They are incredibly loving, energetic, and get along well with children and other pets.



2.Labrador Retriever

Description: A playful and outgoing breed that loves being part of the family. They are highly trainable, active, and friendly to strangers.



3.Beagle

Description: A small-to-medium hound known for its cheerful, curious, and friendly personality. They are great playmates for kids, though they can be stubborn.



4.Shih Tzu

Description: A compact "lap dog" that is affectionate, gentle, and agile. They are excellent for smaller homes or apartments and love to cuddle.
